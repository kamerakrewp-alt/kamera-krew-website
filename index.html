About Kamera Krew

Two souls, one promise — held in frame.

Kamera Krew is a premium wedding photography and films studio founded by Pranit Shambharkar, based in Nagpur and available for weddings worldwide. We chase honest, unscripted moments — the glance before the vows, the laugh mid-Haldi, the quiet second after "yes" — and turn them into images and films you'll return to for a lifetime.
